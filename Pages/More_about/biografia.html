Welcome to Middle-Earth

Here you can find a fan-made page about Middle Earth.
You can learn about many things in this page, but we have divided it into 4 groups:

Regions:

In this chapter we explore the diferent parts of Middle-Earth, going from the Grey Havens to Harad, passing through The Shire, Rivendel, and of course Rohan and Gondor. In each one of them we will discover its people, its traditions and the surroundings in wich they live. We will describe their way of life, their festive days, what is their usual lifespan and how they relate with their enviroment to build their villages.

Main characters:

In this chapter we will explore the diferent main characters of the books. We will divide them into groups depending on their race. About each main character we will talk about how old they where when the books take place, their role in history and known facts about them shuch as their height, their weight or what happened to them after the books.



Battles:

In this chapter we explore the diferent battles that take place in the Lord of the Rings and in the Hobbit in cronological order. Those are: the war of the Last Aliance, the Battle of the 5 Armies, the battle of Helm's Deep, the Siege of Gondor and the Battle of the Morannon. In each battle we will describe who took part in it, how the battle developed, who won and aproximate losses in both sides.




Weapons:

In this chapter we are going to explore the different weapons wielded by our protagonists. We will relate their names, their origin, their original owners if they are known and what battles they have participated in. We will also study the name changes that these weapons have had over the centuries.

This section contains additional information such as a biography of the writter, some media section and a section where you can contact the owner to ask for additional information.
